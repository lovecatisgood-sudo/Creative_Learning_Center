Good to know

Questions parents ask

Clear answers about age, access, memberships, pickup, safety and booking.

The basics

How the club works

What ages can join?

The approved public age range is 5–12. Any exception should be reviewed directly by the team before a visit is confirmed.

Are arrival times fixed?

No fixed class start is required. A family requests a visit window, and the child joins the activity happening on arrival.

Must children stay for the whole session?

No. Stay length depends on the selected pass and confirmed arrangement. Extra-time rules apply if the selected allowance is exceeded.

Is a reservation required?

Reservations are recommended and access is subject to confirmed capacity. Please contact the team to confirm the best arrival window for your preferred day.

Can siblings share a pass?

Passes and memberships are registered per child unless the team confirms a family arrangement in writing.

Is this tutoring?

No. Staff provide homework monitoring, a focus space, encouragement and basic clarification where appropriate. They do not provide formal one-to-one tutoring or guaranteed academic results.

Are cats present?

Cat interaction is separate from Creative Club time and can be discussed with the team, especially for allergy-sensitive children.

Membership & money

Prices, passes and fees

How does the 490 THB first-visit offer work?

It is a three-hour, one-time offer for each new child, subject to confirmed availability and final terms.

How long are Flexi Passes valid?

Validity depends on the selected pass. The team will confirm the current validity before purchase.

What is the late-pickup fee?

Late pickup fees follow the current club policy and are confirmed during registration.

What happens if a child stays beyond the pass duration?

Extra-time fees and rounding follow the current club policy confirmed during registration.

Safety & daily life

Pickup, allergies and care

Who can pick up the child?

Only an authorized person may collect the child under the approved pickup process. The final ID and change-request procedure will be explained during registration.

How are allergies handled?

Basic interest forms do not collect detailed health information. Allergy information is collected securely during registration and severe allergies require direct discussion with the team.

Can staff give medication?

Please discuss any medication need with the team before the visit. Medication support depends on the club accepted process.

Booking

Requests and confirmation

Is a booking request immediately confirmed?

No. The request is confirmed only after the team replies with an accepted date and time.

How quickly does the team reply?

Reply time depends on staff availability and operating hours. The team will respond as soon as possible.

Can a parent visit the space first?

Yes, use signup or contact the team so they can confirm a suitable time.

Can I change the selected plan?

Yes. The team can discuss alternatives with you before confirmation.
