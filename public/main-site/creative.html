Important details

Before joining After School Explorer

  • Parent registration is required before joining.
  • Children must be healthy before attending. Please rest at home for fever, strong coughing or contagious symptoms.
  • This is supervised small-group care, not private one-on-one nanny service.
  • Children under 3 may need a parent or guardian to stay, depending on comfort and safety.
  • Socks are not required in the kids' play area, but socks are required for the cat room.
  • Advance booking is recommended, especially weekends, holidays and evening pickup times.
  • Meal care should be requested in advance when possible.
  • Staff support homework monitoring and quiet focus, but this is not formal one-on-one tutoring.
