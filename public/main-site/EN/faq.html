FAQ

Good to know before booking

Answers for Little Explorer Playgroup, After School Explorer, meal care and booking.

Programs

What is the difference between Playgroup and Creative Club?

Little Explorer Playgroup is for daytime or weekend playgroup care. After School Explorer Program is for after-school routines with homework, creative activity, dinner support and pickup.

Is this private nanny care?

No. Little Explorer Playgroup and After School Explorer are supervised small-group programs, not one-on-one nanny service.

Is this tutoring?

No. Staff can support homework monitoring, reminders, reading and quiet focus, but this is not formal private tutoring.

Prices and meal care

What are the Playgroup prices?

Little Explorer Playgroup starts at 199 THB for 1 hour and 300 THB for 2 hours. Weekday half-day is 599 THB, weekday full-day is 999 THB, and weekend full-day is 1,500 THB.

What are the After School Explorer prices?

After School Explorer starts at 199 THB for 1 hour and 300 THB for 2 hours. Weekday after-school half-day is 599 THB. Meal Care Add-On is 299 THB.

How does meal care work?

Playgroup uses a 250 THB meal-care value for longer-session value. After-school meal care is a 299 THB add-on with one child-friendly food item and one drink.

Safety and booking

Do children need to be healthy before attending?

Yes. If a child is sick, has fever, strong coughing or contagious symptoms, parents should let the child rest at home.

Can children under 3 join?

Children under 3 may need a parent or guardian to stay, depending on the child's comfort and safety.

Are socks required?

Socks are not required inside the kids' play area, but socks are required if entering the cat room.

Are animal visits always included?

No. Cat, rabbit or turtle visits depend on availability, staff schedule, child readiness and animal welfare. All interaction is supervised.

Is advance booking required?

Advance booking is strongly recommended, especially weekends, holidays and evening pickup times.
