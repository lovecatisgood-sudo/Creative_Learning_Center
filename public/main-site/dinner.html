About Siamese Cat Café

Vintage comfort, rescued cats and food made with care

Siamese Cat Café is a warm Western vintage–style cat café in Bang Kaeo near Mega Bangna. It brings together Vietnamese, Thai and Western comfort dishes, signature coffee and desserts, and gentle time with rescued and adopted cats.

The café is family-friendly, open every day, has no entrance fee, and supports animal rescue and medical care. For Creative Club families, it is the meal partner behind eligible dinner-plan meals served during club time.

Allergy and meal records

Tell us clearly before the first meal

Detailed allergy information is collected through the approved secure registration process, not the marketing enquiry form. Parents must update the team when information changes. Severe allergies require a direct discussion about kitchen limitations.

  • No allergen-free guarantee without operational confirmation
  • Meal records follow the approved child profile
  • Parent updates are required when needs change
